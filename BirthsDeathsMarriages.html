GOVERNMENT

  • PROJECT DESCRIPTION:Office fitout
  • LOCATION:Lvl 17, 595 Collins St, Melbourne
  • VALUE$320,000

Description

Renascent were appointed as head- contractor to deliver "Project Refresh" a 6000 m2 facility and services upgrade for 9 Entertainment.This project was carried out over 14 months in total, consisting 5 separate projects, each with their unique site and time challenges. Being a live operational TV station, the planning, services, co-ordination and stakeholder management was enormous. Renascent met every challenge presented, in some instances working around the clock to ensure minimal impact on the station operation.

As part of the complex staging, a section of the second floor spandrel was removed and temporary stairs installed, which allowed major part of the upgrade to be carried out which included substantial demolition, upgrading of all base building services along with new edit suites, toilet upgrades and the construction of new Gateway news studio.
