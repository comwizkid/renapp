Licensed nationally to undertake commercial building works.

Renascent has satisfied all the requirements for state government work and is on the supply register for both NSW and Victorian government work. This enables Renascent to tender and undertake state Government construction, fit-out, refurbishment, design & construct works throughout Victoria and NSW.

Renascent has lodged and has commenced the process of pursuing Office of Federal Safety Accreditation and has undertaken its first audit to achieve accreditation.
