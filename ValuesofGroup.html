Renascent has 4 main core values
that underpin all that we do as an organisation:

Renascent

Col­la­bo­ra­ti­ve

We pro­vi­de the hig­hest level of com­mu­ni­ca­ti­on throughout our pro­ject teams.

We part­ner with cli­ents to solve their needs.

Ac­coun­ta­ble

We are ac­coun­ta­ble to our cust­o­m­ers and are de­pen­da­ble in de­li­ver­ing a qua­li­ty pro­duct.

We pro­vi­de a safe work place and ac­coun­ta­ble for our ac­tions and staff.

Re­spect­ful

We treat our cust­o­m­ers, pro­ject sta­ke­hol­ders and sub-contrac­tors fair­ly.

We pro­vi­de ho­nest ad­vice and gui­dance.

And we act as a re­s­pon­si­ble com­mu­ni­ty mem­ber.

Ex­cel­lence

Our busi­ness is foun­ded on re­peat and sa­tis­fied cli­ents.

We ex­ceed cli­ents' ex­pec­ta­ti­ons in every in­stan­ce.

We con­ti­nu­al­ly stri­ve to im­pro­ve our busi­ness.

Overall, Renascent is an organisation that CAREʼs - we care for our customers, we care for our staff; care for our community. We are an organisation that delivers quality building outcomes that not only are second to none; but ones to which we are proud.
