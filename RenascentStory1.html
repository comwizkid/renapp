01

Renascent was established in 1995 by Andrew Melville and Tony Hargreaves. In 2000
the Melbourne office was established, which has enabled the company to service clients
nationally.

Since inception, under the guidance of Tony and Andrew, the business has experienced
controlled and steady growth culminating in revenues of $100m. The company has strong
financial credentials founded on 17 years of continuous profitability, significant cash
reserves and no debt.

Renascent employs 90 professional staff in its Sydney and Melbourne offices; from head
office administration, cost planning/estimating through to contract administration and project management teams working across all of our projects.

The company's core capability is undertaking construction, fit-out and refurbishment works
and understanding the intricacies of interfacing a construction site within a live operating
environment. Our experience spans across all of the various sectors including general
commercial, state and federal government, hospitality, education and health sectors.
